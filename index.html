I am
Dr. Meghana Rajeev Kore

The founder of Surya Biomedical Waste

It’s a famous saying that says Legacy never dies; it flows through genes from one generation to another. Meghana Kore, the granddaughter of Shri. Ganpatraoji Arwade, has personified the fact that it also takes strength, courage and wisdom to build the legacy. She was just in her early thirties when she took the charge of MRK Group of Companies, established at the hands of Shri. Ganpatraoji, she never looked back and still marching with confidence to take the MRK Group of Companies to the International business sector of Food Processing and Agro Products with confidence and intellectual acuity. Under the spirited leadership of Meghanaji, MRK Group is marching into the future with many great plans and diversifying in many different sectors of Industry like Fish Farming, Construction, Hospitality and more. Like her Grandfather, she is desirous of Education and skill development. While running the business with conviction, Meghanaji is also a loving mother and dependable family member, making her no less than a Superwoman! Born in a business family in the year 1970, Meghanaji was naturally inclined to business activities. Meghanaji witnessed the business skills at home by simply observing her husband Shri. Rajeev Kore grow his construction business high and wide. She completed her Bachelor degree in B.Sc. and moved on to pursue higher studies in Business Management. After completion of MBA, Meghanaji joined the family business in the year 1989 and she is taking care of it since then with zest and zeal. . She took the control of Turmeric Processing Unit and started working on business development plan for the same. After spending hours on study and research, she chalked out a plan to modernize the entire work-flow of Turmeric Processing. She came up with the definitive plan to deploy complete automatic assembly line to produce “Untouched Turmeric”. Biomedical Waste is one the major issues to tackle in urban life. Any waste which is generated during the diagnosis, treatment or immunization of human beings or animals or in research activities pertaining thereto or in the production or testing needs to be taken care of with proper treatment flawless planning and acute implementation of work-flow. In the year 2003, Meghanaji decided to take this challenge and started to work in the field of Biomedical Waste Management for Sangli District. She established Surya Central Waste Management facility and did set an example of how Biological Waste Management can be effectively implemented with cost-effective measures and resources. Soon the Biomedical Waste Management took her to think about Bio Gas. Utilizing her business management skills, Meghanaji decided to diversify. In the year 2007, she started Bio Gas supply as service. Her manifest studious nature and proven business management business ability helped her gain success in every field she entered. John Davison Rockefeller Sr., an American oil industry business magnate and philanthropist, has penned so well: “I don’t think there is any other quality so essential to success of any kind as the quality of perseverance. It overcomes almost everything.” Meghanaji, with her great determination and endurance, continued to unleash different business opportunities that destiny offered her. She continued to grab every opportunity with both hands to help MRK Group of Companies grow in glory.
